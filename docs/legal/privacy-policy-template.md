## Introduction

CivicQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use the CivicQ platform.

**CivicQ is civic infrastructure.** We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We collect only what is necessary to operate a transparent, secure, and accessible voter information platform.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Creation:**
- Email address
- Name
- City/jurisdiction (for residency verification)
- Address (for verification purposes only, not stored permanently)
- Password (hashed and encrypted)

**Verification (Optional for Participation):**
- Address confirmation code (mailed to residence)
- Phone number (SMS verification)
- Date of birth (for voter eligibility verification)
- Government-issued ID (if using third-party verification, processed by vendor, not stored by CivicQ)

**Content You Submit:**
- Questions you submit to candidates
- Votes on question importance (upvote/downvote)
- Candidate answers (if you are a verified candidate)
- Rebuttals and source attachments (candidates only)
- Reports of inappropriate content

**Candidates Only:**
- Candidate filing information (name, race, filing ID)
- Contact information for account verification
- Video recordings of answers
- Transcripts and captions
- Source links and supporting documents

---

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited, features used, time spent
- Device type, operating system, browser
- IP address (anonymized after 90 days)
- Referral source (how you found CivicQ)

**Technical Data:**
- Cookies and similar technologies (session management, security)
- Server logs (for security monitoring and abuse prevention)
- Performance metrics (page load times, errors)

**Video Metadata:**
- Recording timestamps
- Device used for recording
- Video file hash (for authenticity verification)

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Platform Functionality

- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your account, authenticate logins
- **Verification:** Confirm you are a real person and resident of the city/jurisdiction
- **Question Submission:** Allow verified residents to submit and rank questions
- **Candidate Participation:** Enable candidates to record answers and rebuttals
- **Content Display:** Show your questions and candidate answers to the public
- **Moderation:** Review content for compliance with community standards
- **Analytics:** Understand platform usage and improve user experience

### Security and Abuse Prevention

- **Fraud Detection:** Prevent bot accounts, vote manipulation, spam
- **Security Monitoring:** Detect and respond to security threats
- **Audit Logging:** Maintain records for transparency and compliance

### Legal and Compliance

- **Public Records:** Maintain election records as required by law
- **Legal Obligations:** Respond to lawful requests from government authorities
- **Contract Enforcement:** Enforce Terms of Service and community standards

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account information | Until you delete your account | Ongoing service |
| Verification tokens | Until account deletion | Security, fraud prevention |
| Submitted questions | Permanent (public record) | Election archive |
| Candidate videos | Permanent (public record) | Election archive |
| Question votes | Aggregated and anonymized after election | Transparency, research |
| IP addresses and logs | 90 days | Security, abuse prevention |
| Moderation records | 2 years | Appeals, auditing |
| City contract data | Per municipal records retention schedule | Legal obligation |

### Deletion and Data Minimization

- You can delete your account at any time (account data removed within 30 days)
- Questions and votes you submitted become part of the public record and may remain (anonymized)
- Candidates cannot delete published answers (public record principle)
- We automatically delete logs and IP addresses after 90 days
- Verification data is minimized (we store only a hashed token, not your full address or ID)

## Data Security

### How We Protect Your Information

**Encryption:**
- All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Video content signed with cryptographic hashes (tamper detection)

**Access Controls:**
- Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
- Role-based access control (employees only access data needed for their job)
- Regular access reviews and audits

**Monitoring and Response:**
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Incident response plan (breach notification within 72 hours)

**Third-Party Audits:**
- Annual security audits
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (target Year 2)

## Children's Privacy

**CivicQ is designed for adults (18+).**

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 18, you may browse CivicQ as a guest, but you cannot create an account or submit questions without parental consent.

If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## International Users

CivicQ is designed for use in the United States. If you access CivicQ from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from your country.

By using CivicQ, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.
