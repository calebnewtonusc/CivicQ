What is CivicQ?

CivicQ is a civic engagement platform that connects voters with candidates and elected officials. It enables:

  • Transparent Q&A: Voters ask questions, candidates provide public answers
  • Video Responses: Candidates can record video answers to connect with voters
  • Community Voting: Questions are upvoted to surface community priorities
  • Election Management: Cities manage elections, candidates, and ballot information
