Why Answering Matters

Responding to voter questions is one of the most effective ways to:

  • Build Trust: Show voters you're accessible and accountable
  • Earn Support: Candidates who respond get significantly more voter engagement
  • Showcase Your Platform: Use questions to highlight your policy positions
  • Stand Out: Differentiate yourself from candidates who don't engage
  • Reach More Voters: Your answers are shared and can go viral in your community

Data Insight: Candidates who answer at least 80% of questions receive 2.5x more profile views and positive ratings from voters.

Quick Tips for Great Answers

  1. Be Specific: Provide concrete policy positions, not just platitudes
  2. Stay Focused: Directly address the question asked
  3. Use Examples: Share real-world examples or experiences
  4. Back It Up: Cite sources, data, or expert opinions when possible
  5. Add Video: Video responses generate 3x more engagement
  6. Be Authentic: Voters value honesty over perfect answers
