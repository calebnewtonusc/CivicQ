What We Need

To verify your candidacy, please provide one of the following:

  • Official Filing Receipt: Documentation from your county/city clerk showing you've filed to run
  • Campaign Finance Filing: Proof of campaign finance registration (Form 460, 497, etc.)
  • Official Candidate List: Link to official election website listing you as a candidate
  • Ballot Sample: Official ballot or sample ballot showing your name
  • Campaign Website: Link to your official campaign website (will be verified)

Privacy Note: Verification documents are reviewed securely and not made public. Only your verified candidate status will be displayed.

Why We Verify Candidates

Candidate verification ensures:

  • Authenticity: Voters know they're interacting with real, official candidates
  • Trust: Verified profiles build confidence in the platform
  • Accuracy: Election information remains accurate and up-to-date
  • Security: Prevents impersonation and fraud

Verification Process

  1. Submit Documentation: Upload your verification documents (typically takes 2-3 minutes)
  2. Review: Our team reviews your submission (usually within 24 hours)
  3. Notification: You'll receive an email when verification is complete
  4. Go Live: Once verified, your profile goes live and voters can start asking questions

Time-Sensitive: We recommend completing verification at least 60 days before the election to maximize voter engagement.
